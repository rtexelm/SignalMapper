Signal Mapper


A compact web app to mark the locations of amateur radio licensees in the state of New York.


Instructions

Enter a valid zipcode in New York State or choose one of the randomly generated selections to display all callsigns.
Click any of the callsigns to set a marker on the map.
Info describing the license will be shown to the right.
